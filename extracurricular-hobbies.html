Bass Guitar & Band

Bridge to Cross

I started playing bass guitar around 2018-2019, playing an Epiphone Thunderbird bass with an Ampeg amplifier. Music became more than listening for me; it became something I could actively build with other people.

In high school, I played in a local band called Bridge to Cross alongside my friends. We mostly played punk rock covers from bands such as Green Day and Bad Religion, and we also released our own single, "V", as a response to the poor educational system in Romania.
