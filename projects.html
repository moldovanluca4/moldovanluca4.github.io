Hackathon Winning Project - Market Research Automation Agent

Built an award-winning AI automation agent for market research that discovers EdTech/LMS competitors, extracts company websites from blogs and search results, and enriches the dataset with structured LinkedIn information such as company size, headquarters, industry, products, and specialties.

The project also collects forum discussion links from platforms like Reddit and Quora to analyze authentic user opinions, complaints, pros, and cons. The final data is cleaned, consolidated into a SQLite master table, and prepared for integration with n8n workflows and Google Sheets.

The project was designed as a self-contained system where Flask acts as the middle layer between the C executable, the database, and the frontend, making the application feel like one cohesive software product rather than separate scripts.
