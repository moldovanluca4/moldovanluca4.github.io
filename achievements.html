2026 Mitacs Globalink Research Internship Award

Selected by Mitacs and Professor Qingrun Zhang at the University of Calgary to receive a 2026 Mitacs Globalink Research Internship award and undertake a research project at the University of Calgary.

This is a competitive international research award that matches top-ranked students with Canadian faculty supervisors for a 10 to 12 week project. Selection recognizes a strong match between the student's skills, education, and research experience and the specific contribution they will make to the host research project.

The funding grant is co-administered by Mitacs and DAAD-RISE Worldwide in partnership with the University of Calgary.
